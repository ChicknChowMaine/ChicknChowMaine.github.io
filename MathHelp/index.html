Course Outline

Assuming your course is anything like mine was, the examination is broken down into two seperate papers, Paper 2 and Paper 4. These are 1 hour and 30 minutes, and 2 hours and 30 minutes long, respectively. Paper 2 contains more stand alone, short answer questions, whereas Paper 4 contains more structured equations, as in the solution to one problem may be required to solve the following question.
